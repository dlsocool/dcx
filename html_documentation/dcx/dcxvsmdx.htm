Divider    
 
The divider control in MDX and DCX differ so much all they really have in common is the name.
  In DCX a divider is a moving bar that you "dock" 2 controls to. By moving this bar the docked childs are automatically sized. Its also possible to have a divider, in a divider, in a divider, etc. In MDX the divider is a sizing bar that you can move around and in combination with MoveControl and $calc you can adjust control's sizes.
